Our Vision & Values

At ROH, our vision is to ensure every rural student in Canada has equal access to scholarships, opportunities, and mentorship. We value inclusivity, accessibility, and community support.

Our Story

ROH was founded to bridge the rural access gap in education. Too often, students in rural communities struggle to find scholarships or programs that are tailored to their unique needs. We set out to change that by building a centralized, student-friendly hub where resources are easy to find.
